Ingredients

  • 1 pizza crust
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup diced green bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup sliced pepperoni

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450Â°F.
  2. Spread tomato sauce evenly over pizza crust.
  3. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over tomato sauce.
  4. Add black olives, mushrooms, green bell pepper, red onion, and pepperoni on top of cheese.
  5. Bake pizza in preheated oven for 10-12 minutes or until crust is golden brown.
  6. Remove from oven and let cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
