About Us

We are a family-owned and operated pizza restaurant that has been serving delicious, authentic Italian pizza since 1995. Our pizza is made with the freshest ingredients and cooked to perfection in our wood-fired oven.

In addition to our pizza, we also offer a variety of pasta dishes, salads, and desserts to satisfy any craving. We take pride in providing our customers with a warm and welcoming atmosphere and excellent service.
